Mission

We made this website for our Kode with Klossy Final Project, and we hope that it can be useful to you. Our Mission is to inform voters, and citizens participating in the 2020 presidential election to become more knowledgable about each candidate running, help reveal their stance, and hopefully to encourage everyone of legal age to get out there, and vote for a president who will best represent you. We hope you can use our website to look more into candidates for the election and take action to vote.

Topics

In our page we will mainly cover 3 topics and the Candidates stance. We will be focusing on Gun Control, Immigration and Education.
